## What this example demonstrates

- Full audit before any writes — user sees gap taxonomy with counts.
- User picks specific gap types (phone + email only) — no unwanted writes.
- Batched confirm: one approval for the whole batch, not per-item.
- Bounded execution: 70 writes, 5 concurrent, under 500 cap.
- Before/after log published as a doc for auditability.
- Safety rail held: no deletes, no amount-column writes, no cross-workspace moves.
- `Generated by Claude` footer + `<!-- claude-skill-id: data-cleanup -->` for idempotency.
- Remaining gaps surfaced with next-step suggestions.
